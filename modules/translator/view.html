WHAT IS PDF FIX?
Old Hindi PDFs (Krutidev, Chanakya, Shivaji fonts) store text as garbled Latin characters. When you copy and paste, you get broken text instead of proper Hindi.
PDF FIX repairs the encoding → gives you proper Devanagari → then translates to English.
⚠ Works best with 1-2 paragraphs at a time.
STEP 1 — PASTE GARBLED PDF TEXT
Supports Krutidev encoding (most common — govt docs, newspapers, old publications). AI applies the complete Krutidev→Devanagari mapping table for accurate conversion. Works best with 1-2 paragraphs at a time.
